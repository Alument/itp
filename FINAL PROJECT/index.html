About

I am a music producer from NYC currently living in Boston. I've been studying music since I was three, and producing since I was 12. I specialize in electronic music, but there isn't a genre I won't dabble in. I've done a very large amount of digital synth design, so I can make anything from Leads and soft Pads, to hard basses, growls... etc. I currently make music for a fitness organization where I combine different styles of world music with tranditional house/dance.
